SSO ID Rajasthan - Key Information

SSO ID is your one-stop digital key to access various online government and private services offered by the Rajasthan Government. Below is a quick overview of important details about the SSO ID system:

What is SSO ID?

SSO ID stands for Single Sign-On Identity. It is a digital ID created by the Rajasthan Government to access many online government services using one login.

SSO ID Rajasthan (Single Sign-On ID) is a digital identity system introduced by the Government of Rajasthan to streamline access to various government services through a single platform. Launched in 2013 by the Rajasthan State Electronics Development Corporation (RSDC) under the Department of Information Technology, this initiative aims to simplify and digitize public service delivery for citizens, businesses, and government employees.

Who Launched SSO ID?

The SSO ID Rajasthan was started by the Government of Rajasthan.Specifically, it was created and managed by the Department of Information Technology and Communication (DoIT&C), Rajasthan.

The goal was to make it easier for people in Rajasthan to use government services online without needing many different accounts.
This project helps the government provide services faster and in a more organized way, supporting the idea of a Digital Rajasthan where everything is connected through technology.

The organization behind the development and management is the Rajasthan State Electronics Development Corporation (RSDC), which works to bring digital services to all citizens, businesses, and government employees in the state.

📌 Frequently Asked Questions

What is SSO ID Rajasthan?

SSO ID Rajasthan is a unique digital ID provided by the Government of Rajasthan that allows users to access various online government services using a single login. It works as a unified platform where citizens, businesses, and government employees can register and use services such as job applications, e-Mitra, bill payments, and official documents without needing multiple accounts. It helps promote digital governance and saves time by offering centralized access.

How to create new SSO id?

To create a new SSO ID, visit the official Rajasthan SSO portal at https://sso.rajasthan.gov.in/register/. On the registration page, choose the appropriate user type: Citizen, Udhyog (Business), or Government Employee. Citizens can register using their Jan Aadhaar, Bhamashah ID, Google account, or Facebook account. Government employees must register using their SIPF ID, while businesses can use their BRN (Business Registration Number) or GSTIN. After selecting the suitable option, fill in the required details like name, mobile number, email ID, and verify your identity using OTP. Once verified, create a username and password to complete your SSO ID registration.

Who can register for an SSO ID?

Anyone who is a resident of Rajasthan, owns a business in the state, or is a government employee working under the Rajasthan state government is eligible to register for an SSO ID. Citizens can register using their Aadhaar, Jan Aadhaar, Bhamashah ID, or even social accounts like Google and Facebook. Government employees can sign up using their SIPF number.

What documents are required to create an SSO ID?

To register for an SSO ID, you generally need one of the following: Jan Aadhaar ID, Aadhaar card, Bhamashah ID, or SIPF ID (for government employees). You should also have a valid mobile number linked to your ID and, optionally, an email ID. These details are used for verification and receiving OTPs during registration.

How do I reset my SSO ID password?

If you forget your SSO ID password, go to the official website (sso.rajasthan.gov.in), click on the “Forgot Password” link, and follow the steps. You’ll be asked to enter your registered mobile number, email, or Aadhaar number to receive an OTP. Once verified, you can create a new password.

Can I update my Jan Aadhaar ID in SSO ID?

Yes, you can easily update or link your Jan Aadhaar ID in your SSO profile. Just log in to your SSO account, go to your profile or settings, and choose the “Update Jan Aadhaar” option. Enter your Jan Aadhaar number and verify it using the OTP sent to your registered mobile number. After successful verification, your ID will be updated.

What services can I access using SSO ID?

With your SSO ID, you can access over 100 services including e-Mitra, RPSC/RSSB job portals, Bhamashah, Jan Aadhaar services, bill payments, scholarship forms, SIPF for employees, Rajasthan Sampark (grievance portal), MSME and business registrations, and more. It’s a one-stop solution for most government-related tasks.

Is there a mobile app for SSO Rajasthan?

Yes, the SSO Raj mobile app is available for Android users on the Google Play Store. It allows users to access services, update their profile, and manage their account directly from their smartphones, making it convenient for people who don't use computers regularly.

What should I do if the SSO portal is not working?

If the portal is not loading or showing errors, try clearing your browser cache, switching to another browser, or checking your internet connection. If the issue still persists, you can call the helpline number 1800 180 6127 or email helpdesk@rajasthan.gov.in for technical support.

Is SSO ID registration free?

Yes, creating and using an SSO ID is completely free. There are no registration charges or hidden fees for accessing any of the government services through the SSO portal.
